                </li>
                <li>
                  <i>Who is a Person With Disability </i>
                  <div class="answer-paragraph">
                    <p>A Person With Disability is person who has a long-term physical, mental, intellectual or sensory 
                    impairment which in interaction with various barriers may hinder their full and effective participation 
                    in society on an equal basis with others. [IRR of RA 10754]</p>
                  </div>
                </li>
                <li>
                  <i>What is the law governing Senior Citizen Discount and Person with Disability Discount?</i>
                  <div class="answer-paragraph">
                    <p>The law governing Senior Citizens discount is Republic Act No. 9994 (RA 9994), or the Expanded 
                    Senior Citizens Act of 2010, amending RA Nos.7432 and 9257, while the law governing the rights and 
                    privileged for Persons With Disability is RA 10754, or An Act Expanding the Benefits and Privileges 
                    of Persons with Disability, amending RA Nos. 7277, 9442 and 10070.</p>
                  </div>
                </li>
                <li>
                  <i>What are the products and services that a senior citizen/person with disability entitled to a discount?</i>
                  <div class="answer-paragraph">
                    <p>The products and services that a senior citizen and person with disability entitled to the grant of a discount are:
                    <ol>
                      <li> 20% discount and value-added tax (VAT) exemption on the purchase of: 
                        <ul style="list-style: disc;">
                          <li>Medical-related privileges:</li>
                            <ul style="list-style:square;">
                              <li>Medicine and drugs purchases</li>
                              <li>Essential medical supplies, accessories and equipment</li>
                              <li>Medical and dental services</li>
                              <li>Professional fees of license professional health providing home health care services</li>
                              <li>Professional fees of attending physicians</li>
                            </ul>
                          <li>Domestic transportation privileges</li>
                          <li>Fare for public land, domestic air, and sea travel</li>
                          <li>Hotels, restaurants, recreational centers, and places of leisure, and recreation center</li>
                          <li>Admission fees charged by theaters, cinema houses and concert halls, circuses, leisure and amusement</li>
                          <li>Funeral and burial services</li>
                        </ul>
                      </li>
                      <li>5% special discount of the regular retail price, without exemption from the value-added tax (VAT), of basic necessities and prime commodities (BNPCs), listed in Section 2 (a) and (b) of the Joint Administrative  Order (JAO) No. 24-02, Series of 2024 or the “2024 Revised Rules on Granting Special Discounts to Senior Citizens and Persons with Disability on Purchase of Basic Necessities and Prime Commodities”</li>
                    </ol> <br>
                    It is noted that the discounts is for the exclusive use and enjoyment or availment of the senior citizen and person with disability.
                  </p>
                  </div>
                </li>
                <li>
                  <i>What is the basis of the 5% special discount for senior citizens and persons with disability?</i>
                  <div class="answer-paragraph">
                    <p>The basis of the special discount to senior citizens and persons with disabilities is <b>Section 4 (j)</b> 
                      of RA 9994 or the “Expanded Senior Citizens Act of 2010”, and <b>Section 32 (d)</b> of RA 7277 as amended, 
                      which states that “to the extent possible, the government may grant special discounts in special programs 
                      for senior citizens on the purchase of basic commodities, subject to the guidelines to be issued for 
                      the purpose by the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI) and the Department of Agriculture (DA).”</p>
                  </div>
                </li>
                <li>
                  <i>What is Joint DTI-DA-DOE Administrative Order No. 24-02?</i>
                  <div class="answer-paragraph">
                    <p>Joint DTI-DA-DOE Administrative Order No.24-02 or the “2024 Revised Rules on Granting Special 
                      Discounts to Senior Citizens and Persons With Disability on Purchase of Basic Necessities and 
                      Prime Commodities (BNPCs)” is an amendment of Joint Administrative Order No. 17-01 and 17-02, series 
                      of 2017 increasing the Special Discounts to Senior Citizens and Persons with Disability on 
                      their purchase of BNPC considering inflation, among others. <br> <br>
                        
                      The aforementioned JAO came into effect on 25 March 2024.</p>
                  </div>
                </li>
